Rinsoft Web & Mobile Technology Experts

Rinsoft is a software design company that creates customized applications for web, mobile and social media platforms. We bring more than 30 years of combined experience and a consultative, business-building approach to every assignment. From elementary schools to colleges, healthcare startups to major medical centers, multi-platinum rock bands to Fortune 50 corporations, Rinsoft serves a broad range of organizations across multiple industries.

Suresh Mohanakrishnan, Co-Founder

Suresh Mohanakrishnan studied Computer Science at Cal State, Northridge and has more than 17 years experience in software development, platform architecture and project management.

Suresh has created applications for startups in numerous industries and for major corporations such as Bank of America. Staying at the forefront of technology trends, he specializes in the latest applications for enterprise including mobile and HTML5 solutions. Suresh is a Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP).

Kashif Noor, Co-Founder

Kashif Noor studied Computer Science at Cal State, Northridge and has more than 17 years experience as a solution architect, lead engineer, developer and analyst.

Kashif has designed and implemented systems for a wide array of companies including Fox Networks, American Honda Motors, Intuit and Bank of America. He specializes in cross platform, cross browser and multi-technology experiences. Kashif is a Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP).
