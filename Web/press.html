Rinsoft releases Reader Buddy V1.1.2 to iTunes App Store

Calabasas CA – Nov. 16, 2012 – Rinsoft, LLC. today released Reader Buddy, the latest version 1.1.2 of the company’s iPhone app that will allow Elementary school children or their parents to track their reading times. The logs can then be emailed to the teacher or parents to be printed.

The app offers a conveniant bar code reader to allow users to simply scan the barcode of the book to capture it’s info. Once the book information is captured a students reading times can be logged for that book.

Reader Buddy is free to downloaddownload from the iTunes store.

The only reading logs app in iTunes

This application was designed by parents of young children who need to capture their reading habits. The book and log information is always at their fingertips and can easily be shared with the students teachers via email. said Suresh Mohan, co-founder and managing partner at Rinsoft, LLC.

The app will also allow parents to review the books read by their children. The reviews can be posted on their Facebook page to help other parents learn about the book.
